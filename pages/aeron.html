Aeron: A New Biome

ABOUT_ME
Code is under Digital Bros Game Academy ownership

What is it?

My first Unreal Engine project and the last one made in academy (the biggest one, also called "The Big One").
The game is a puzzle stealth third person game. In a far solarpunk future Vicktor is an engineer and is sent by its company SolarLife on Aeron, a planet being terraformed in order to host humans. Something went wrong and the robots in charge of the terraforming process started behaving strangly. Vicktor has to make its way by solving puzzles, avoiding being killed by corrupted robots to unveil the mistery of Aeron.

When was it made?

First half of 2024, developed in 6 months

Who made it?

A team of 17 people:
  • 5 Game Designers
  • 4 Game Programmers (including me as Lead)
  • 3 Concept Artists
  • 5 3D Artists

What did I learn/practice?

Unreal Engine and C++

What is it?

Rubik cube game recreated in Unity with integrated solver

When was it made?

During 2018 summer. Core developed in 1 month, refinements through the years. My second Unity project, the first completely made on my own.

Who made it?

Me on my own

What did I learn/practice?

  • I practiced and got into details of some aspects of the engine such as cameras, lights, models, materials, animations, sounds and scenes
  • At a later time I tried to optimize the game as much as I could so I learned how to use the Profiler and the URP render pipeline by converting the project to this last one
